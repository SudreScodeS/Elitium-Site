What is an ERP
and why do you need one?

ERP stands for "Enterprise Resource Planning" — a system that centralizes all your company\'s management in one place.

Stop using separate spreadsheets

Instead of having your sales in one spreadsheet, inventory in another, and finances in a notebook — Elitium brings it all together in a single dashboard. You see the complete picture of your business in real time.

With integrated artificial intelligence, the system doesn\'t just organize your data — it also analyzes trends, forecasts demand and helps you make better decisions. e ajuda você a tomar decisões melhores.

And if you\'re a developer: documented REST API, automated tests and CI/CD included.

Segurança

Security by design

Every layer of the system has its protection. It\'s not an extra feature — it\'s part of the foundation.

JWT with Rotation

Short-lived access tokens (1h) with rotating refresh tokens. If a token leaks, it expires quickly.

HttpOnly Cookies

Refresh tokens in HttpOnly cookies — JavaScript can\'t access them. Protected against XSS.

CSRF Protection

All data-modifying operations are protected against forgery attacks.

Rate Limiting

Automatic request control per IP and user. Prevents abuse and brute force attacks.

Access Control

Granular roles (admin/seller) with module-specific permissions. Each person sees only what they need.

Audit Logs

Complete record of all actions. Who did it, when, what. Full traceability.
